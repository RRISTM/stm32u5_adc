# ADC resolution

The adc can be set to different resolution

## ADC1/2

Resolution up to 14bit
This will influence a conversion time

| Resolution | Conversion periods | 
|------------|--------------------|
| 14 bit     | 17 ADC CLK         | 
| 12 bit     | 15 ADC CLK         | 
| 10 bit     | 13 ADC CLK         | 
| 8 bit      | 11 ADC CLK         |


## ADC4

Resolution up to 12bit

| Resolution | Conversion periods | 
|------------|--------------------|
| 12 bit     | 12.5 ADC CLK       | 
| 10 bit     | 10.5 ADC CLK       | 
| 8 bit      | 8.5 ADC CLK        | 
| 6 bit      | 6.5 ADC CLK        |
